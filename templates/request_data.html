Frequently Asked Questions
How long does it take?

We typically process requests within 30 days. Complex requests may take up to 90 days.

Is there a cost?

No, requests are free. We may charge for additional copies if you request them.

What if I don't get a response?

If we don't respond within the timeframe, you can lodge a complaint with your local Data Protection Authority.

Can I get the data in another format?

Yes, you can request JSON, CSV, or other portable formats. Let us know your preference above.
