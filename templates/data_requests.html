About Subject Access Requests

A Subject Access Request (SAR) is your right under GDPR Article 15 to request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.

What you'll receive:
  • All personal data linked to your account
  • Information about where it came from
  • How it's being used
  • Who it's been shared with
  • Your data rights and options
Timeline:
  • Requests are typically processed within 30 days
  • Complex requests may take up to 90 days
  • You'll be notified when your data is ready for download
