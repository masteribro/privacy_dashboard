Data Processing & Legal Information

1. Data Controller and Processor Information

PrivacyFirst Dashboard acts as a Data Controller for data collected directly through our platform.

  • Controller Name: PrivacyFirst Dashboard
  • Contact: dpo@privacyfirst.demo
  • Data Protection Officer: privacy@privacyfirst.demo
  • Registered Address: Demo Privacy Operations Center, Demo Country

2. Processing Activities

PrivacyFirst processes the following categories of personal data:

  • Account Information: Username, email, encrypted password
  • Connected Data Metadata: Organisation names, data source types, connection timestamps
  • Data Items: Categories, names, purposes of your personal data
  • Consent Records: Consent status, granted/withdrawn dates
  • Activity Logs: User actions, IP addresses, timestamps
  • Preferences: Language, theme, notification settings

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), we process personal data on the following legal bases:

  • Legitimate Interest (Article 6(1)(f)): For security, fraud detection, and service improvement
  • Consent (Article 6(1)(a)): For newsletter subscriptions and additional analytics
  • Legal Obligation (Article 6(1)(c)): For compliance with privacy laws and regulations
  • Contract (Article 6(1)(b)): To provide the PrivacyFirst Dashboard service

4. Automated Decision Making and Profiling

PrivacyFirst uses automated decision-making for:

  • Privacy Health Score Calculation: A non-binding score calculated from your consent status and data holdings
  • Anomaly Detection: Automatic flagging of unusual account activities for security

You have the right to request human review of any automated decisions affecting you.

5. Data Recipients

Your personal data is shared with the following recipients:

  • Internal Staff: Only Privacy and Data Protection team members can access user data for support
  • Service Providers: Cloud hosting providers with Data Processing Agreements in place
  • Legal Authorities: If required by law enforcement or court order
  • NOT SHARED: Marketing companies, data brokers, or third-party advertisers

6. International Data Transfers

PrivacyFirst Dashboard stores data within the European Economic Area (EEA). If you access from outside the EEA, data may be transferred to the EEA. Transfers are conducted in compliance with GDPR Chapter V safeguards.

7. Data Retention Periods

We retain your personal data for the following periods:

  • Account Data: For the duration of your account + 30 days after deletion
  • Audit Logs: 7 years for compliance and investigation purposes
  • Subject Access Requests: 3 years from request date
  • CCPA Opt-out Records: Retained as required by CCPA (minimum 12 months)
  • Activity Logs: Last 100 entries visible; older logs archived for 2 years

8. Your Data Protection Rights

Under GDPR and CCPA, you have the following rights:

  • Right of Access (GDPR 15, CCPA): Request a copy of your data via Subject Access Request
  • Right to Rectification (GDPR 16): Correct inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure (GDPR 17, CCPA): Request deletion of your data
  • Right to Restriction (GDPR 18): Limit processing of your data
  • Right to Data Portability (GDPR 20, CCPA): Download your data in structured format
  • Right to Object (GDPR 21): Object to certain processing activities
  • Right to Withdraw Consent (GDPR 7, CCPA): Withdraw consents and opt out
  • Right to Non-Discrimination (CCPA): We do not discriminate for exercising rights

9. How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of your data protection rights:

  • Subject Access Request: Use the "Request Data" feature in your account
  • Opt-out (CCPA): Use the "CCPA Settings" page to opt out of data sales or sharing
  • Withdraw Consent: Use the "Consents" page to manage your consents
  • Other Requests: Email dpo@privacyfirst.demo with your request

10. Data Security Measures

PrivacyFirst implements the following security measures:

  • End-to-end encryption for data in transit (SSL/TLS)
  • Encryption of sensitive data at rest (AES-256)
  • Secure password hashing (bcrypt)
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Comprehensive access controls and audit logging
  • Incident response procedures with 72-hour breach notification

11. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach affecting your personal data, we will:

  • Notify you within 72 hours as required by GDPR
  • Provide details of the breach and affected data
  • Explain measures taken to secure your data
  • Provide contact information for questions

12. Third-Party Links

The PrivacyFirst Dashboard may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their privacy policies before providing personal data.

13. Data Protection Authority

If you believe your data protection rights have been violated, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local Data Protection Authority:

  • EU/EEA: Your national Data Protection Authority
  • UK: Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) - ico.org.uk
  • California: California Privacy Protection Agency (CPPA)

14. Contact Our Data Protection Officer

For any questions about how we process your data:

  • Email: dpo@privacyfirst.demo
  • Postal Address: Privacy Operations, PrivacyFirst, Demo Country
  • Response Time: We respond to all inquiries within 14 days
