Data Coming IN (From Organizations)
From Organizations:
  • Personal Data - Name, email, phone, address
  • Financial Data - Account details, transactions, balance
  • Behavioral Data - Purchase history, browsing, preferences
  • Health Data - Medical records, appointments, allergies
Security: All data flows are encrypted and use secure protocols
Data Going OUT (Your Control)
You Can:
  • View all data held about you
  • Export data in JSON/CSV format
  • Opt-out of data sales (CCPA)
  • Withdraw consents at any time

Privacy Recommendations
✓ Do This:
  • Regularly review what data is collected about you
  • Withdraw consents for services you don't use
  • Exercise CCPA opt-out rights if you're a CA resident
  • Download your data periodically for personal records
  • Check audit logs for suspicious activity
Don't Do This:
  • Share your account credentials with anyone
  • Enable data collection you don't understand
  • Ignore privacy policy updates
  • Use the same password on multiple services
  • Assume all data collection is legitimate
