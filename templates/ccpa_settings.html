Your CCPA Rights
  • Right to Know: You can request what personal data organisations collect, use, and share about you. Submit Request
  • Right to Delete: You can request deletion of personal data collected about you (with some exceptions).
  • Right to Opt-Out: You can opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal data. Use the forms above to exercise this right.
  • Right to Correct: You can request correction of inaccurate personal data held about you.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: Organisations cannot discriminate against you for exercising your CCPA rights.
For more information about CCPA, visit ccpa-info.ca.gov or our Data Processing page.
