Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

PrivacyFirst Dashboard is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our platform. This Privacy Policy explains our online information practices and the choices you can make about how your information is used.

2. Information We Collect

The PrivacyFirst Dashboard collects information to help you manage and understand your personal data held by various organisations:

  • Account Information: Username, email address, and password when you register
  • Connected Data: Information about your data connections with organisations
  • Data Items: Personal information shared through your connected organisations
  • Consent Records: Information about the consents you have given or withdrawn
  • Activity Logs: Records of your interactions with the platform for security and compliance

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the PrivacyFirst Dashboard service
  • To enable you to manage your personal data and consents
  • To comply with legal obligations (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)
  • To improve and personalise your experience
  • To maintain security and prevent fraud
  • To fulfil Subject Access Requests and other data rights

4. GDPR Rights (EU Users)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the following rights:

  • Right of Access: You can request a copy of your personal data through our Subject Access Request feature
  • Right to Rectification: You can request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: You can request deletion of your data
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You can limit how we use your data
  • Right to Data Portability: You can download your data in a structured format
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: You can withdraw consents given for data processing

5. CCPA Rights (California Residents)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California residents have the following rights:

  • Right to Know: You can request what personal data is collected, used, and shared
  • Right to Delete: You can request deletion of personal data collected from you
  • Right to Opt-Out: You can opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal data
  • Right to Correct: You can request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate for exercising your CCPA rights

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. All data transmission is encrypted using SSL/TLS protocols.

7. Consent Management

The PrivacyFirst Dashboard is designed to help you manage and control the consents you have given to organisations. You can withdraw any consent at any time through your Consents dashboard page.

8. Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. Audit logs may be retained for compliance purposes as required by law.

9. Third Parties

The PrivacyFirst Dashboard does not sell your personal data. We may share information with service providers who assist us in operating the platform, subject to confidentiality obligations. We do not share your data with marketing partners.

10. International Data Transfers

The PrivacyFirst Dashboard is hosted within the European Economic Area (EEA). If you are accessing this service from outside the EEA, your data may be transferred to the EEA in accordance with applicable laws.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the service constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

  • Address: Privacy Operations, PrivacyFirst, Demo Country
